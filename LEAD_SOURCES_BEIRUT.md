## Priority Order
1. Clinics and offices (high need for stable network)
2. Cafes and restaurants (public Wi-Fi pain)
3. Gyms and shops (camera/POS connectivity issues)
4. Schools and training centers

## What To Capture Per Lead
- Business name
- Contact number or social link
- Area
- Main likely issue (Wi-Fi drops, POS downtime, CCTV bandwidth)
- Next action date
