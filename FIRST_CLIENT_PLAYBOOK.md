# First Client Playbook (48 Hours)

## Mission
Book and close 1 paid networking job in 48 hours.

## Main Offer (do not change)
"Free 15-minute network check for offices/homes in Beirut/Keserwan. If I find an issue, I send a fix plan + price."

## Paid Packages (anchor pricing)
- Package 1: Wi-Fi Rescue Visit - 60 USD
  - Fix weak signal, dead zones, unstable speed.
- Package 2: Security Hardening - 90 USD
  - Router firewall, password policy, guest network isolation.
- Package 3: Full Network Audit - 120 USD
  - Traffic check, bottlenecks, topology cleanup, action report.

## Non-Negotiable Daily KPIs
- 40 direct outreaches/day (WhatsApp + LinkedIn + Instagram)
- 10 direct calls/day
- 10 follow-ups/day
- 2 booked checks/day

## 48-Hour Sprint Plan
### Block 1 (Hour 0-1) - Setup
- Set status line in all profiles:
  - "Network Engineer | Beirut | 14+ years | Free 15-min network check | WhatsApp +96170841009"
- Keep 3 proof photos ready (router rack, AP setup, cable/fiber).
- Open `LEADS_TRACKER.csv` and keep it open while sending.

### Block 2 (Hour 1-3) - First Wave
- Add 20 leads from `LEAD_SOURCES_BEIRUT.md`.
- Send first 20 messages using `WHATSAPP_SCRIPTS.txt`.
- Mark status instantly after each send: `SENT`.

### Block 3 (Hour 3-5) - Second Wave
- Add 20 more leads.
- Send second 20 messages.
- Call any lead who viewed/replied without booking.

### Block 4 (Hour 5-7) - Close Attempts
- Use two-slot close only:
  - "I can do quick check today 6 PM or tomorrow 11 AM. Which one suits you?"
- Offer urgency only once:
  - "If we book today, I include free signal map check."

### Day 2 (Repeat + Follow-up)
- Follow up all no-replies after 24h.
- Post one proof-based story and one short CTA post.
- Push every warm lead to a time slot, not a vague promise.

## Objection Handling (copy/paste)
- "Not now"
  - "No problem. The check is 15 minutes only and helps avoid random spending on wrong devices. Better today 6 PM or tomorrow 11 AM?"
- "How much?"
  - "Most fixes are 60-120 USD depending on issue. I confirm exact price after free check."
- "We already have internet provider"
  - "Perfect. I work on internal Wi-Fi stability, dead zones, and security settings that providers usually do not optimize."

## Closing Lines
- "Fastest safe fix for your setup is [X]."
- "Total is [price] USD including setup, optimization, and testing."
- "I can start [Option A] or [Option B]."

## Execution Rules
- Do not send long technical paragraphs.
- Always end with one direct CTA question.
- Follow up at 6 hours and 24 hours.
- Sell outcome (stable connection, less downtime), not brand names.

## Proof Lines
- "14+ years in network installation and troubleshooting."
- "Worked on enterprise and SMB deployments."
- "Focus: stable Wi-Fi, secure access, less downtime."
